Barnes and Noble Commercial

When I was first learning how to edit video, I decided to take on a project that I was a little out of depth for. In this video, I didn't use an external mic when I recorded (big mistake) and spent a lot of time editing the audio to get to sound okay. I also don't particularly like how I transititoned each clip to the next and the different angles I shot the clips in. However, this is a project I wanted to showcase on my portfolio anyway because it shows that I know how to write a script, storyboard my idea, and bring it to life! If I were to recreate this video, I would do things differently but that just goes to show that editing is all about practice!

"How To" Video

For another project, I had to create a "how to" video. This video is "How to Create a Venti Flat White With Latte Art". I personally think this video is edited better than my other example, however the film quality was not great (I didn't have a tripod). As you watch this video, you may wonder: "why am I watching this clip when there's nothing happening?" and the answer is because I was trying fluff my way into the minimum time requirement. Also I can't do latte art, I tried really hard to learn for this video but I couldn't do it- that's why it just cuts to the coffee at the end without showing you how to do it.
